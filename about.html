ABOUT THE WOMEN'S DEVELOPMENT CELL

Established in 2015, The Women's Development Cell at Shri Ram College of Commerce is a progressive community driving change through research, impactful projects, and collaborations. We strive to break down obstacles, promote empowerment, and make equality a reality. Together, we aspire to establish a secure and equitable atmosphere in which everyone can thrive, as well as to encourage individuals to challenge preconceptions, accept diversity, and lead compassionately.

OUR OBJECTIVES

The Women’s Development Cell, SRCC is dedicated to promoting women empowerment and gender sensitization. It strives to encourage women’s leadership in academics, entrepreneurship, and professional fields. The cell aims to raise awareness about menstrual health and challenge taboos through campaigns, discussions, and sanitary pad donation drives.

Through its magazine, “Beyond Pink,” WDC fosters dialogue on gender issues, feminism, and social change, encouraging students to express their perspectives. Committed to awareness, advocacy, and meaningful change, the Women’s Development Cell remains persistent in its efforts to build a more balanced, inclusive, and empowered society.
