RISE ABOVE THE RIBBON

22nd October 2024

In contribution to the Breast Cancer Awareness Month, WDC SRCC organized the Rise Above the Ribbon Seminar, featuring Dr. Deepika Chauhan. The seminar included an engaging discussion on the small steps one can take toward better health, including how to avoid carcinogenic elements.

Impact & Outreach

Dr. Chauhan also emphasized that breast cancer was not discriminatory based on gender. This seminar marked the conclusion of Breast Cancer Awareness Month, ending the month with maximum awareness dissemination among students who attended both online and offline.
