Annual Symposium Schedule

Our Annual Symposium brought together leaders, students, and academics for a day of insightful discussion and collaborative learning. Below is the schedule of the day's events.

10:00 AM

Opening Remarks

Welcome address by the President of WDC, SRCC, setting the stage for the day's discussions.

11:00 AM

Keynote by Dr. Aisha Verma

An inspiring talk on "Women in Leadership" and breaking corporate glass ceilings.

01:00 PM

Panel Discussion

A multi-disciplinary panel on the role of policy in achieving gender equity, followed by a Q&A session.

03:00 PM

Interactive Workshop

A hands-on workshop on financial literacy and independence for young women.

The symposium concluded with a networking session, fostering connections and inspiring future collaborations among attendees. The positive feedback highlighted the event's success in promoting meaningful dialogue.
