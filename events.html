EVENTS

VEERANGANA

Veerangana, the flagship event of the Women Development Cell at Shri Ram College of Commerce, is a platform dedicated to celebrating and empowering women. This event brings together accomplished women, role models, and change-makers who share their journeys, challenges, and achievements.

View More

PERIOD PAIN SIMULATOR

The Period Pain Simulator was an interactive initiative designed by women development cell, SRCC in collaboration with pinkishe foundation to raise awareness about what it feels like to experience this pain while also creating empathy within those who do not know what it is like to have to survive it.

View More

PAD DISTRIBUTION DRIVE

The Women's development cell SRCC joined forces with safeOsure to distribute menstrual pads under it's initiative "Flow support" in MalkaGanj slums, New delhi on 13th September 2023. This drive was organized with the aim of educating young girls and women about menstrual hygiene management while providing free sanitary pads to those who cannot afford them.

SEMINARS

The Women Development Cell (WDC), SRCC takes immense pride in organizing informational seminars that not only provide an avenue to raise awareness among students but also build a safe space for fruitful interaction. These seminars create a healthy forum for discussion, helping to break barriers on sensitive topics.
