EVENTS

VEERANGANA

Veerangana, the flagship event of the Women's Development Cell, is an empowering initiative celebrating and promoting women's strength and resilience. The event brings together inspiring speakers, interactive workshops, and engaging activities that foster leadership skills and promote gender equality among students.

PERIOD PAIN SIMULATOR

An innovative and educational initiative designed to foster empathy and understanding by allowing participants to experience simulated menstrual cramps. This event aims to break down the stigma surrounding menstruation and create a more inclusive and supportive community for all genders.

View More

PAD DISTRIBUTION DRIVE

A community-focused initiative where we distribute sanitary napkins to underprivileged women and girls. This drive not only provides essential hygiene products but also includes awareness sessions on menstrual health, aiming to ensure dignity and health for every woman.
