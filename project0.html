A Conversation with the Project Lead

On the "Art for Empowerment" Initiative

What was the inspiration behind this project?

We wanted to create a platform for women to express themselves through art. The idea was to use creativity as a tool for healing, storytelling, and building community among students who might not otherwise connect.

What were some of the outcomes?

We hosted a final exhibition that was attended by hundreds of students and faculty. The powerful stories shared through the artwork sparked important conversations about identity and resilience. More importantly, the participants formed a strong, supportive network that has continued long after the project ended.
