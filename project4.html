Research Wing: Gender Pay Gap Analysis

An in-depth analysis of the prevailing gender pay gap in India's corporate sector, published by our dedicated Research Wing.

Our latest research provides a comprehensive overview of the economic disparities faced by women in the workplace. The study delves into statistical data from various industries, highlighting the systemic issues that contribute to the gender pay gap. We explore factors such as unconscious bias in hiring and promotions, the motherhood penalty, and the underrepresentation of women in high-paying roles and sectors.

The paper also presents a series of policy recommendations for corporations and government bodies aimed at mitigating these disparities. These include promoting pay transparency, implementing unbiased recruitment processes, and providing better support for working mothers. Our goal is to contribute to academic discourse and drive meaningful policy changes for a more equitable future.
