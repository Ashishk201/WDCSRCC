Financial Literacy Drive

Empowering women with the knowledge to achieve financial independence.

Project Overview

The Financial Literacy Drive was a multi-workshop project designed to equip young women with essential financial skills. We covered topics ranging from budgeting and saving to investing and understanding credit. The goal was to demystify personal finance and provide actionable steps towards building a secure financial future.

Our sessions were interactive, featuring guest speakers from the finance industry and hands-on activities. The positive feedback highlighted a strong demand for such initiatives, and we plan to expand this project to reach a wider audience in the coming year.

Key Topics Covered

  • Budgeting & Saving
  • Introduction to Investing
  • Understanding Credit Scores
  • Navigating Student Loans

Project Impact

  • 200+ Participants
  • 5 Interactive Workshops
  • 95% Positive Feedback
