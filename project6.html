Campus Safety Initiative

This initiative aims to improve safety and awareness on campus through workshops, policy advocacy, and resource distribution. Here are some frequently asked questions about the project.

The primary goal is to create a safer and more inclusive campus environment for all students by addressing issues of harassment, promoting bystander intervention, and improving access to support resources.

All workshops are open to all students of the college, regardless of gender. We encourage everyone to attend to foster a community of shared responsibility.

We are working with the college administration to review and update existing safety policies. Our research team has submitted a proposal with recommendations based on student surveys and best practices from other institutions.
