"Beyond Pink" Magazine Launch

Phase 1: Conception

Ideation and Team Formation

The project began with brainstorming sessions to define the magazine's theme and vision. A dedicated editorial and design team was formed from student volunteers.

Phase 2: Content Creation

Call for Submissions & Editing

A call for articles, poetry, and artwork was sent out to the student body. The editorial team then curated and edited the submissions to ensure high quality and thematic coherence.

Phase 3: Launch & Distribution

Magazine Launch Event

The magazine was launched at a special event featuring readings from contributors. Digital and print copies were distributed across campus, receiving excellent feedback for its insightful content and design.
