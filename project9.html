Case Study: Digital Literacy for Senior Women

Bridging the digital divide for the senior women in our community.

The Challenge

Many senior women in our local community felt isolated due to a lack of digital skills, making it difficult to connect with family, access services, and stay informed. This project aimed to address this digital divide directly.

Our Solution

We organized a series of free, hands-on workshops tailored to the needs of beginners. Student volunteers provided one-on-one guidance on topics including:

  • Using smartphones and video calls.
  • Navigating social media to connect with loved ones.
  • Accessing online banking and government services safely.

The Results

The program successfully trained over 50 senior women, many of whom reported a significant increase in confidence and a stronger sense of connection to their community. The project fostered inter-generational bonds between students and participants, creating a truly rewarding experience for everyone involved.
