OUR PROJECTS

Project Aasha

A comprehensive initiative focused on spreading awareness about menstrual health and hygiene in underprivileged communities. We conduct workshops and distribute sanitary products.

Project Synergy

Fostering collaboration between students and corporate leaders to mentor and guide young women in their career paths, focusing on leadership and skill development.

Research Wing

Our dedicated research division that publishes papers and articles on pertinent gender-related issues, contributing to academic discourse and policy-making.
