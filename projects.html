OUR PROJECTS

Project Code Red Alert

At WDC, SRCC, this flagship initiative tackles one of the most overlooked aspects of student well-being, menstrual health. Through interactive workshops, communication modules, and social media campaigns, we’re dismantling stigma and normalizing conversations around periods for school-going children. And we’re not stopping there; we’re expanding this change to factory floors, ensuring every woman gets access to awareness and dignity.

Project Synergy

Through Synergy, we channel skills in marketing, finance, and operations into consulting and HR live projects that drive social change. By partnering with organizations like Ahanaa Heritage Foundation and Aspire For Her, we merge professional expertise with purpose, empowering initiatives dedicated to women’s development.

Project Saanjh

Saanjh is our umbrella project under which meaningful initiatives take shape—free pad distribution drives, self-defense workshops, book donations, visits to old-age homes, and skill enhancement programs. Each effort under Saanjh is a thread weaving stronger communities and brighter futures.
